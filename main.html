Travel


San Francisco International Airport (SFO)

SFO is the closest international airport. It’s about 1 hour and 45 minutes driving to/from Guerneville. We will set up a way for folks to connect with other guests to carpool.


Santa Rosa Airport (STS)

STS is a small airport located 20 minutes east of Guerneville. However, it has much more limited flight options.
