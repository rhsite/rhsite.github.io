Accommodations

Guerneville offers a range of lodging options for the wedding weekend. We encourage everyone to book early, as this is a popular area that fills all lodging during peak season!

General context that might inform lodging choices:

  • During the wedding weekend, we will provide refreshments and meals from Friday evening to Sunday morning.
  • The venue is a 15-minute walk from downtown Guerneville.
  • It can get hot in May - check listings for A/C if you think you'll need it.

On-site camping/glamping

The wedding party has exclusive access to a full-service RV campground adjoins the venue. It includes showers and bathrooms, and ample space for everyone who wants to camp in an RV or tent. Quiet hours will be at 10pm every night. To reserve a camping spot, select the camping/RV prompt on the RSVP form.

If you don’t have gear and are interested in camping (yay!), reach out to us and we can facilitate borrowing or renting gear. Furthermore, local company A Fun RV (☎️ 707-585-8431) can set up an RV for you at the campground beforehand, and pick it up after you leave. The cost is $550-700 and they work with this campground often.

Travel

San Francisco International Airport (SFO) is the closest international airport, 1h30 from the wedding. Oakland (OAK) and San Jose (SJC) are also feasible. Santa Rosa Airport (STS) is a small domestic airport 20 minutes away.

There is limited public transit from the Bay Area, and ample parking at the venue. In the leadup to the event, we will distribute a voluntary carpool signup sheet.
